about_CommonParameters

Common parameters are parameters handled by the shell, and work for every command (that havent disabled them).

They exists to give global functions across crosshell, like show information and likewise.

Help:

The help parameter (/help) runs the command thru the get-help cmdlet. And shows information about a commmand ant it's parameters.

/? -? and /h are aliases for the help parameter.
